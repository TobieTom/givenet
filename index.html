How It Works

Simple steps to start sharing in your neighbourhood.

1

List an Item

Describe and post the items you want to donate. Add photos, set your location and preferred collection method.

2

Connect with Neighbours

Recipients browse available items and send requests. You review and accept the right match.

3

Hand Over Safely

Arrange pickup or delivery and mark the item as collected. Leave feedback to build community trust.
