Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of any page. Fill in your basic information including name, email, and password. Once verified, you can start browsing and posting items immediately.

Yes. GiveNet is completely free for all users. There are no hidden fees, subscription charges, or transaction costs. We're a community-sharing platform built to help people, not to make money from them.

After logging in, go to your Donor Dashboard and click "Post New Item." Upload clear photos of your item, write a description, select the category, indicate the condition, and specify your pickup preferences. Your listing will be reviewed within 24 hours before going live.

When you send a request, the donor receives a notification with your message. They'll review your request and either approve or decline it. If approved, you'll hear from them to arrange pickup. Communication happens through our secure messaging system and both parties are kept in the loop.

Always meet in public, well-lit locations like shopping centres, cafes, or community centres. Let someone know where you're going and when to expect your return. For larger items, consider meeting at a safe exchange zone. If something feels wrong, cancel the meeting and report any concerns.

Yes. Go to "My Items" in your Donor Dashboard. Click the edit icon on any listing to update details, or the delete icon to permanently remove it. If you've already received requests, please notify those requesters before deleting an item.

We take community safety seriously. If you encounter any issues, use the "Report" button on the item listing or user profile to alert our moderation team. We review all reports within 24 hours. For urgent safety concerns, contact us directly at support@givenet.com.

After completing a successful exchange, both parties can leave ratings on a 1–5 star scale. Ratings are an important trust signal in the community. All reviews are moderated to ensure they're fair and appropriate.

If an item you've requested is no longer available, you'll be notified. You can browse other similar listings or set up alerts for items in a specific category. We encourage donors to keep their listings up to date to avoid disappointment.
