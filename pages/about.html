About GiveNet

Building Stronger Communities through Giving

GiveNet was founded on a simple belief — that generosity has the power to transform communities. We connect people who want to give with people who need it most, making sharing easy, safe, and meaningful.

Join Our Community

Our Mission

Connecting communities through the act of giving

We're on a mission to build platforms that unite people of all walks of life through a shared sense of community. GiveNet believes that every item shared brings people closer together, reduces waste, and creates a culture of mutual care where everyone can thrive.

Whether you're donating something you no longer need or finding an item that transforms your family's day-to-day, GiveNet makes it simple, safe, and dignified for everyone involved.

What we stand for

These values guide every decision we make and every feature we build.

Dignity First

Every exchange on GiveNet is built on respect. We ensure donors and recipients interact with dignity and care.

Trust & Safety

Our moderation team reviews all listings and handles reports to maintain a safe environment for everyone.

Local Support

We believe change starts locally. GiveNet focuses on connecting people within the same community.

Community-Focused

We're not just a platform — we're a movement. Every decision we make puts our community members first.

What we stand for

The principles that shape how we build and serve our community every day.

Dignity

We treat every person on our platform with respect, regardless of what they give or receive.

Fairness

Community
