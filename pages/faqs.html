Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using GiveNet safely and effectively.

Getting Started

Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner, fill in your name, email, and password, and verify your email address. You'll be able to start browsing and posting items as soon as you're verified.

Yes, completely. GiveNet is free for all users with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or transaction charges. All items must be given freely — any monetary exchange violates our terms.

Log in, go to your Donor Dashboard, click "Post New Item," add photos and details, and submit. Our admin team reviews the listing and makes it live within 24 hours once approved.

Donating Items

You'll need clear photos, an honest description of the item's condition, category selection, and your preferred pickup arrangement. Items must be clean, functional, and safe to use.

You can donate clothing, furniture, electronics, books, baby items, kitchenware, toys, and more. Items must be in usable condition. We do not allow weapons, hazardous materials, live animals, or illegal items.

Yes. Go to "My Items" in your dashboard to edit or delete any listing. If you've received requests, please let those users know before removing the listing.

Listings stay active until you manually remove them or mark them as collected. We recommend removing items as soon as they're no longer available to avoid frustrating recipients.

Yes, there's no limit on how many items you can donate. Just create a separate listing for each item to make it easy for recipients to find what they need.

Requesting Items

Browse available items and click "Request Item" on any listing you're interested in. Write a short message explaining why you'd like the item, then submit your request. The donor will review and respond.

You can request multiple different items, but you're limited to 3 active requests at a time. This ensures fairness across the community and gives everyone a chance to receive items.

If your request is declined, you'll receive a notification. The item may still be available for someone else to request. You're free to search for similar items and request again.

Donors typically respond within 24–48 hours. If you haven't heard back within 3 days, you may want to reach out to our support team or continue browsing for similar items.

Pickup & Exchange

Always meet in public, well-lit locations such as shopping centres, cafes, community buildings, or designated safe exchange zones. Avoid meeting at private residences unless you know the person well.

If an item is significantly different from its description, you are not obligated to accept it. Report the discrepancy to our team using the Report button on the listing so we can review the donor's account.

For large items, bring help to carry them. Have a bag or box for smaller items. It's also a good idea to let someone know where you're going and bring a charged phone.

Safety & Trust

All listings are reviewed before going live. Our rating system keeps all users accountable. You can report any user or listing at any time, and our moderation team reviews reports within 24 hours. Repeat offenders are permanently banned.

Leave the situation immediately and contact local authorities if necessary. Then report the incident to us at support@givenet.com or use the in-app Report feature. Your safety is our top priority.

Yes. Use the Report button on any listing or user profile to flag inappropriate behavior. You can also email us at support@givenet.com. All reports are confidential and reviewed promptly.

Account & Settings

Go to your Profile settings from the dashboard navigation. You can update your name, profile photo, location, and contact preferences at any time.

Yes. You can switch between Donor and Recipient roles from your dashboard. The "Switch Account" option in your profile allows you to toggle between both roles with a single click.

To delete your account, go to Profile Settings and select "Delete Account." Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your listings and history will be removed.

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page and enter your email address. We'll send you a reset link. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes.

Troubleshooting

First, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, contact our support team at support@givenet.com with a description of the issue and any error messages you're seeing.

Make sure your image is under 5MB and in JPG, PNG, or WEBP format. Try a different browser or device. If you're on mobile, ensure GiveNet has permission to access your photo library.

Email us at support@givenet.com with a description of the bug, the steps to reproduce it, your device type, and browser version. Screenshots are very helpful. We aim to fix critical bugs within 24 hours.
