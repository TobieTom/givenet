Community Standards 👥

Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure a positive experience on GiveNet.

Safe Meeting Practices

  • Meet in Public Places: Choose well-lit, busy locations like coffee shops, shopping centres, or community centres.
  • Bring a Friend: When possible, bring someone with you to the pickup or exchange, and let someone know where you're going and when to expect your return.
  • Meet During Daylight: Schedule pickups during business hours whenever possible to avoid meeting strangers in the dark.
  • Trust Your Instincts: If something feels wrong, cancel the meeting and report your concerns to our team.

Protecting Your Information

  • Use Platform Messaging: Keep communication within GiveNet's messaging system until you're ready to exchange contact details for pickup coordination.
  • Don't Share Financial Info: Never share bank details, credit card numbers, or other financial information with other users.
  • Limit Personal Details: Only share necessary information for coordinating the pickup. Avoid sharing your home address publicly.
  • Check User Profiles: Review ratings, reviews, and account history before agreeing to meet.

Item Quality Standards

  • Only donate items that are clean, functional, and safe to use.
  • Accurately describe the condition. Include photos of any wear or damage.
  • Check for product recalls before donating electronics or children's items.
  • Package fragile items carefully for safe transport.

Warning Signs to Watch For

  • Pressure to Act Quickly: Be wary of users who pressure you to meet immediately or make rushed decisions.
  • Requests for Money: GiveNet is free. Anyone asking for payment or shipping fees is violating our terms.
  • Unwillingness to Meet Publicly: If someone insists on meeting in an isolated or private location, decline.
  • Inconsistent Information: Watch for contradictions in descriptions, photos, or details about the item.
  • Poor Communication: Users who are evasive or unable to answer reasonable questions should be approached with caution.

Reporting Concerns

  • Report suspicious listings or users immediately using the Report button.
  • If you feel unsafe at any point, leave and contact local authorities.
  • Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours.
  • Repeat offenders are permanently banned from the platform.

Quick Safety Tips

Simple steps for a secure, respectful, and dignified community exchange.

Verify Items

Review items carefully before accepting them. Ensure they match the description and photos provided.

Safe Locations

Prefer locations that have "safe exchange zones" specifically for online transactions.

Report Issues

If you encounter inappropriate behavior, report it immediately to our moderation team.

Feel Unsafe? Report It

If you feel threatened or unsafe at any time, contact local authorities immediately. Then report the incident to our safety team at support@givenet.com or use the in-app reporting feature.
