How GiveNet Works

How GiveNet Works

Making it simple to give and receive items in your community. A few easy steps connect donors with recipients every day.

Get Started Now

For Donors

How to donate an item

Share what you no longer need and make a real difference in someone's life.

1

List Your Item

Upload photos and write a description of your item. Set the category, condition, and preferred pickup method. Your listing is reviewed before going live.

2

Receive Requests

Community members browse available items and send you a request with a message. You choose who receives your donation based on their message.

3

Stay Connected

Communicate with the recipient through our secure messaging system to arrange a convenient pickup time and location.

4

Mark as Collected

Once the item has been picked up, mark the request as collected. Leave a rating to help build community trust.

For Recipients

How to request an item

Find the items your household needs, for free, from generous neighbours nearby.

1

Browse Available Items

Search and filter items by category, condition, or location. Find what you need from our growing catalogue of donated items.

2

Request an Item

Submit a request with a short message explaining why you'd like the item. The donor reviews your request and approves the right match.

3

Arrange Pickup

Once approved, coordinate pickup details with the donor through our messaging system. Choose a safe, public location that works for both of you.

4

Confirm Pickup

After collecting the item, the donor marks it as collected. Leave a rating to help maintain the community's trust and accountability.

Your Responsibilities

As a Donor

  • Only list items that are clean, functional, and safe to use.
  • Respond to requests within a reasonable time frame.
  • Be honest and accurate in your item descriptions.
  • Meet recipients in safe, public locations for pickup.
  • Mark items as collected once the exchange is complete.

As a Recipient

  • Write a thoughtful, honest request message.
  • Show up on time and communicate any changes promptly.
  • Treat donors with respect and gratitude.
  • Only request items you genuinely need.
  • Leave an honest rating after each exchange.

Trust & Community Safety

Built on trust, driven by community

GiveNet takes the safety of every exchange seriously. All listings are reviewed by our admin team before going live, and every user profile can be reported if something feels off.

Our rating system holds everyone accountable. Donors and recipients rate each other after every transaction, so you always know who you're dealing with.
