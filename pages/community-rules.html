Community Standards 👥

Community Rules

These rules keep GiveNet a safe, welcoming space for everyone.

DO
  • Be respectful and courteous in all interactions.
  • Provide accurate descriptions and photos of your items.
  • Respond to requests and messages promptly.
  • Meet in safe, public locations for pickups.
  • Report suspicious activity or listings.
  • Keep your profile information up to date.
Don't
  • Don't list items for sale, GiveNet is for free donations only.
  • Don't discriminate against any user based on background or identity.
  • Don't share personal contact details in public listings.
  • Don't post duplicate or spam listings.
  • Don't misrepresent item conditions or availability.
  • Don't use abusive, threatening, or inappropriate language.

Violations

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent removal from GiveNet. We review all reports fairly and aim to resolve issues within 24 hours.
